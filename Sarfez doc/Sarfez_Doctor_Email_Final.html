Heart failure patients often avoid discussing OAB with their physicians

 

A recent study found nearly half (41%) of heart failure patients prescribed loop diuretics did not report OAB symptoms, such as urgency and incontinence to their physicians. Many of these patients suffer in silence and accept the negative impact this has on their quality of life.

 

Communicating with patients about worsening OAB symptoms with the use of a loop diuretic and offering alternative treatment options could help alleviate their embarrassment and improve their daily routine.
